Who We Are

Barkk is a telecommunication healthcare app for animals. For many pet owners, it can be hard to determine when it is necessary to bring your pet into a local veterinarian. Many times, an insignificant issue with your dog or cat can lead to spending hours in the waiting room and a large bill to conclude a simple visit that couldve been treated at home. Every pet owner wants the best for their animal, and sometimes it is hard to tell when a discoloration, a scratch, or a loss of appeite is a more serious problem than it seems. Barkk is a company that provides veterinary services through the phone and in the comfort of your own home. Barkk offers a 24/7 live chat feature. If you're needing more, you can schedule video appointments with professional and experienced veterinarians who can answer all your questions.
